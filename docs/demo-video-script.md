## TECHNICAL RECORDING NOTES

### Quest Recording:
- Use Quest's built-in recording feature
- Record at highest quality (1080p minimum)
- Ensure smooth framerate (30fps minimum)
- Capture clear audio

### Mobile Recording:
- Use screen recording app (iOS Screen Record, Android Screen Record)
- Show full screen, not just app
- Demonstrate touch interactions clearly
- Record in portrait or landscape (landscape preferred)
